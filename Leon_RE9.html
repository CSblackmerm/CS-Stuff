Leon S Kennedy

Resident Evil Requiem/9:

Leon S Kennedy is one of two main characters in Resident Evil Requiem with the other being named Grace. In this game you go back and forth between sections in which you play as Leon and sections in which you play as Grace, with there being more Grace sections in the being and there being more Leon sections later on in the story. This is done to try and bleed together the more survival horror focused version of Resident Evil with Grace and the more action horror side with the Leon sections.

This game was released in 2026
