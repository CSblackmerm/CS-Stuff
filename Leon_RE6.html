Leon S Kennedy

Resident Evil 6:

Leon S Kennedy is one of the many main characters of Resident Evil 6. With the game itself being made up of four main campains, and the game itself being a single player to duo player co-op. In Leon's campain he's paired up with other main character named Helena. With Leon also making appearences as you play through the other campains as the storys overlap with eachother.

This game was released in 2012
