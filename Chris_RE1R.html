Chris Redfield

Resident Evil:

Chris Redfield is the one of two main protagonists in the original Resident Evil game with the other being Jill. This character is usally considered as being the harder of the two characters to play as as yes you do get the lighter in your free inventory slot and due to how the animations were made a slightly faster attack speed, not only do you have two less inventory slots, you also don't get the lockpick tools that Jill gets, locks you out of certain doors early game as well as requiring you to use your inventory slots to hold keys instead. Unlike all the other remake games based on the games of this era, this game keep the fixed cmera angles and tank controls.

This game was released in 2002 as a remake to the original that was released in 1996
