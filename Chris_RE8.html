Chris Redfield

Resident Evil Village/8:

Chris Redfield is a side character in Resident Evil 8, with him appearing in the intro cutsceen, being the main person to kick off the events of the game, then later him appearing around the point fourth main boss fight against Karl Heisenberg, then a little later in the game there is a section were you play as Chris storming the village the game takes place in, and finally for the main story he appears in the ending cutsceen after the main boss is defeated. In side content he appears again as a playable character. This is the second game in the main series to be first person.

This game was released in 2021
