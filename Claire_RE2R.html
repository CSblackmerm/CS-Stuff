Claire Redfield

Resident Evil 2:

Claire Redfeild is one of the two protagonists in Resident Evil 2. With the game having 4 main branches to tell it's story with Both Claire and fellow protagonist Leon having both an A route and a B route that depends on who you choose to play as first at the start of the game. This is a remake of the original RE2 that came out all the way back in 1998.

This game was released in 2019
